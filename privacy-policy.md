# Privacy Policy for LockedIn

Last updated: May 18, 2026

## Overview
LockedIn is a Chrome extension that helps users block distracting websites 
during user-defined time ranges. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
LockedIn collects only the following data:
- URLs of websites you choose to restrict
- Date and time ranges you set for each restriction

## How We Use Your Data
The data collected is used solely to provide the core functionality 
of the extension — blocking websites during the times you set. 
Your data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising or personalization.

## Data Storage
Your website restriction preferences are stored securely in a 
MongoDB Atlas cloud database. All data is encrypted at rest.

## Data Deletion
You can delete any of your website restrictions at any time directly 
through the extension. This permanently removes the data from our database.

## Third Parties
We do not share your data with any third parties.
